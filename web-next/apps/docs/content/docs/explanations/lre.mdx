---
title: Local Remote Execution
description: Hermetic builds on your laptop — no Docker, no containers, no surprise dependencies.
---

Local Remote Execution (LRE) is NativeLink's answer to "how do I get the
hermeticity guarantees of remote execution without giving up the iteration
speed of building locally?"

The short version: NativeLink runs a remote-execution worker on your machine
and routes every action through it. Inputs are content-addressed, the
toolchain is pinned by Nix, and the result is bit-for-bit reproducible across
machines.
